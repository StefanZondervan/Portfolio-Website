Face Frame

I embarked on a creative journey driven by experimentation and the desire to explore the boundaries of sensory perception. The core idea behind this project was to craft a unique face-based frame that could both enhance and restrict our senses, leading to novel sensory experiences.

Inspiration for this project sprang from the theoretical realm of "ex machine," where we contemplate the future of human interaction with the body and emerging technologies. I saw an opportunity to delve into this intriguing concept and offer a glimpse into a future where our senses and perceptions could be altered in captivating ways.

The creative process began with countless sketches and rudimentary prototypes. These initial experiments helped me refine the design and functionality of the sensory frame. I envisioned a form-fitting frame that could be comfortably worn on the face, allowing users to attach various sensory attributes with ease. Magnets became the key to this innovative concept, allowing for the effortless attachment and removal of sensory modifiers.

The standout feature of this project is the ability to wear the frame, designed to contour the face naturally. It serves as a canvas for the attachment of sensory attributes, transforming the way users perceive the world around them. By donning this frame, individuals can enhance or block specific senses like sight, smell, or hearing, offering a unique and immersive experience.

Materials played a pivotal role in bringing this concept to life. Fabric, metal, and magnets were carefully chosen to ensure durability, comfort, and ease of use. These materials allowed for the creation of multiple sensory attributes, each with its own design and style, catering to a variety of sensory experiments.

Building this project presented its share of challenges, particularly as I had limited experience working with metal. However, through determination and a willingness to learn, I overcame these obstacles. The result is a well-crafted frame that is both functional and user-friendly, ready to be embraced by those eager to explore the realms of altered sensory perception.

While this project served as an experimental and fun endeavor, it also showcased the versatility of my creative and artistic capabilities. It is a testament to my willingness to push boundaries and explore innovative concepts in the realm of sensory experiences. Through this project, I invite others to join me on this imaginative journey into the future of human interaction with the senses, where possibilities are limited only by our creativity.
